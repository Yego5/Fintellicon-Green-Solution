National Tree Planting Day

Kenneth Kiplagat, one of our esteemed directors, joined Sochoi Primary School during National Tree Planting Day, showcasing our commitment to environmental sustainability and community development. This event underscores the importance of reforestation and environmental conservation in the fight against climate change. Tree planting is a simple yet powerful act that contributes to restoring degraded ecosystems, improving air quality, and fostering biodiversity. By involving young learners from Sochoi Primary School, the initiative also instills a sense of environmental stewardship and responsibility in the next generation. National Tree Planting Day serves as a reminder of the collective role we all play in safeguarding the planet for future generations.
